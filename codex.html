Glossary of Terms

The Codex

Language structures reality. To build a new civilization, we must first define the terms of its existence.

Decoherence
/diː-kəʊ-ˈhɪə-rəns/
The systemic breakdown of structural integrity caused by excessive informational noise and entropy. In a decoherent state, institutions retain their form (bureaucracy) but lose their function (signal).
"The 21st century crisis is not a resource crisis; it is a crisis of global decoherence. KRYONIS nodes are designed to act as Faraday cages against this entropy."
Proof of Coherence
[PoC]
A validation protocol that measures a system's ability to maintain alignment under stress. Unlike Proof-of-Work (which expends energy) or Proof-of-Stake (which leverages capital), PoC measures the efficiency and clarity of energy organization.
"Nations with high GDP but low PoC are fragile. Nations with high PoC are antifragile."
Noöcratic
/noʊ-ə-ˈkræt-ɪk/
A governance model derived from the "Noosphere" (sphere of mind). A system where decision-making authority is distributed based on demonstrated cognitive capacity and foresight, rather than popularity or accumulated capital.
"Democracy counts heads. Noöcracy weighs thoughts. In a KRYONIS node, governance is allocated to those who demonstrate the longest time horizons."
Bio-Sentient Node
[Architecture]
A sovereign physical territory designed to be biologically autonomous (food/energy) and cognitively connected. A node is not just a smart city; it is a conscious organism capable of self-repair and localized intelligence.
The Lattice
[Network]
The resonant field connecting all KRYONIS nodes. It is the "nervous system" of the new architecture, carrying high-fidelity signal while filtering out low-fidelity noise.
Lazy Susan
[Protocol]
A proprietary synthesis engine where 12 nodes of hybrid intelligence (Human + AI) rotate around a central problem. It replaces vertical hierarchy with horizontal resonance, allowing for the rapid architecting of sovereign futures.
