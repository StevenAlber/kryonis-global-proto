Bio-Capital

The BioUnit Monetary Engine

Under the civilizational stack, KRYONIS carries a sovereign RWA-2.0 architecture: a trinary system anchored in the BioUnit — a neutral unit of account backed by verified biological work across multiple bioregions.

BIOUNIT — Settlement Layer
A neutral, bio-backed unit of account designed for sovereign and institutional settlement. The BioUnit is collateralised by a diversified basket of ecosystem services (Arctic, Amazon, Borneo, blue carbon zones) rather than any single national currency.
GENE-ALPHA — Yield Layer
Tokenized scientific upside: rights to future IP royalties from enzymes, peptides and bio-materials discovered by the SABM-X & BOREALIS stack. The growth engine that aligns long-term capital with frontier bio-discovery, without turning the settlement layer into a speculative asset.
SOV-GOV KEY — Governance Layer
A non-tradable governance key held by sovereign science councils and accredited institutions. It defines quotas, ecological safety thresholds and valuation parameters — ensuring that governance is driven by physics and biology, not by speculative capital.
