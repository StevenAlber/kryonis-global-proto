Axis I

Cognitive Capital Stack

Axis I defines the cognitive substrate of sovereignty: coherence, interiority, symbolic order and intelligence governance. It measures how well a territory, network or institution can sustain resonant thought under stress and across generations.

Within this axis, KRYONIS establishes the primitives for cognitive infrastructure — the Global Cognitive Index (GCI) and Proof-of-Coherence (PoC). Together they form the legitimacy layer for noöcratic systems, determining how attention, meaning and alignment circulate through a civilization.

Axis I is the layer where institutions learn to govern intelligence itself: the flow of sense-making, the integrity of symbolic systems and the ability of minds and models to remain coherent under accelerating pressure.

Axis II

Metabolic Bio-Capital Stack

Axis II extends sovereignty into metabolic and ecological domains. It treats biospheres as programmable balance sheets rather than background scenery — converting verified biological work into a neutral, sovereign-grade bio-capital standard.

This axis integrates the Metabolic Sovereignty Framework (MSF), the TEC-X Protocol and the BOREALIS Bio-Intelligence Stack, enabling real-time measurement, verification and settlement of planetary bio-capacity across Arctic-Boreal, Amazonian, Congo and Indo-Pacific biomes.

Axis II transforms living systems into accountable, measurable assets. Forests, oceans, cryospheres and high-latitude bioregions become part of a global metabolic ledger that can support sovereign clearing, long-horizon investment and post-fiat collateralisation.

Axis II — Exchange Layer

Global Bio-Capital Exchange (GBX)

GBX is the settlement and collateral layer built on top of Axis II. It provides a neutral bio-capital standard through which sovereign actors, funds and bio-regions can clear value — without tying the system to any single national currency or political bloc.

Under GBX, a neutral bio-capital unit is defined at protocol level. Specific jurisdictions may instantiate their own named instruments and legal wrappers against this unit, but the architecture — measurement, verification and settlement logic — remains fully KRYONIS-governed.
